## Post Content

Everyone's chasing Reddit for AI visibility.  
But there's a bigger opportunity most are ignoring.

YouTube.

Google is still the dominant search engine, and that's not changing anytime soon. AI Overviews and AI Mode are how most people will experience LLM results going forward.

Fair or not, Google is going to pull its own ecosystem from when building those responses, and YouTube is the crown jewel of that ecosystem.

Reddit gets a lot of attention when you think about LLM visibility generally because it's text-heavy and LLMs are clearly trained on it.

AI Overviews aren't just about training data, it's what Google trusts and surfaces right now. Look at this chart from Semrush Enterprise, and you can see YouTube visibility taking off. This is why.

YouTube content is:

- Indexed by Google directly
- Tied to authority signals Google already measures
- Transcribed and readable by AI systems
- Preferred in queries with how-to or explainer intent

If you want your brand, product, or ideas showing up in Google's AI responses, YouTube is one of the most underrated levers you have.

Stop spamming Reddit and focus on building a YouTube audience.

YouTube takes more effort, which is exactly why the opportunity is still wide open.
