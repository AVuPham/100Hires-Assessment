## Post Content

Unsolicited #SEO tip: Appearing in the answers for AI surfaces is not so much a matter of optimising your own site, as that as multiple other sites. Last year, several "SEO friends" all posted the same/very similar "top SEO experts" list, and it comes up again and again when I see people use AI to generate these lists.

Explore the fanout/web searches that happen in the background, get on those sites, and your visibility in AI answers will be much more stable than just trying to get a page on your site to be cited.
