Every time someone runs a Cora report, anonymous correlation data gets sent back to the network. That data is aggregated across thousands of real SERPs and published publicly.

No opinions. No blog myths. Just measurements.

Number of Referring Domains? #35.

The site also tracks factor classes — from Relevancy (54,123 factors) to Authority (163) to Trust (15).
